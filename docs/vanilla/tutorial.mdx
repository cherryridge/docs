# Vanilla Tutorial

This is a very brief and simple tutorial for the Vanilla content pack. In this page, we will cover the basics of Vanilla, such as the goal of the game, basic mechanics and its difference from Minecraft.

## Goal

Just as "a good sandbox game has no goal", there is no end goal in Vanilla. But the general goal is to build a civilization, upgrade to better technology, and eventually do whatever you want to be your goal. The possibilities are truly endless.
